(1) on or after the appointed date, no banking business shall be carried on except by a company under the authority of a licence issued by the monetary board with the approval of the minister. (2) where such company is a company incorporated outside sri lanka no licence shall be issued unless such company has complied with the provisions of part xiii of the companies act no. 17 of 1982.
