(8) (a) a cancellation of a licence of a licensed commercial bank under subsection (3) shall take effect— (i) where the bank does not prefer an appeal against such cancellation under subsection (4), on the expiration of the period for tendering such appeal; or (ii) where an appeal has been preferred against such cancellation, on the confirmation of the decision of the monetary board by the court of appeal or the supreme court, as the case may be; (b) a cancellation of a license of a licensed commercial bank under subsection (7), shall take effect from the date of the receipt by the bank of the notification under that subsection.
