Self-assessments. 132. (1) A self-assessment taxpayer who has filed a self-
assessment return in the approved form for a tax period shall
be treated, for all purposes of this Act, as having made an
assessment of the amount of tax payable as set out in the
return (including a nil amount) for the tax period to which
the return relates.

set out in the return.

in writing or electronically by a taxpayer shall be a self-
