Short title and date of
operation 1. (1) This Act may be cited as the Banking Act, No. 30 of 1988.
[ * 16th September,1988]
(2) The provisions of this Act other than Part V shall come into operation on
such date*as may be appointed by the Minister by Order published in the Gazette
(hereinafter referred to as the ― appointed date ‖).
[*20th September 1988] (3) The provisions of Part V of this Act shall come into operation on such
later date* as may be appointed by the Minister by Order published in the Gazette.
PART I
LICENSING OF PERSONS CARRYING ON BANKING BUSINESS
Licensing of banks. 2. (1) On or after the appointed date, no banking business shall be carried on
except by a company under the authority of a licence issued by the Monetary
Board with the approval of the Minister.
(2) Where such company is a company incorporated outside Sri Lanka no
licence shall be issued unless such company has complied with the provisions of
Part XIII of the Companies Act No. 17 of 1982.
(3) Any company, which has been issued a licence under the provisions of this
Part, shall, hereinafter, be referred to as a ― licensed commercial bank ‖ (4)
Notwithstanding the provisions of subsections (1) and (2) of this section and of
section 3 of this Act, all commercial banks specified in Schedule I to this Act,
which on the appointed date are carrying on banking business, shall be issued a
licence in accordance with the provisions of this Act.
